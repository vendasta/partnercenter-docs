# Is there a way to remove an account from an Automation?

Once an account has been processed through automation, it cannot be undone. However, if the automation includes a delay step, you may have a brief window of time to intervene and stop the process. If no delay step is present, the automation will complete almost instantly, leaving no opportunity to interrupt it.
