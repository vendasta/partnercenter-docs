# Can I create Proposals in multiple currencies?

If you sell in multiple currencies, you may have already set up multiple markets in your account with select packages, your preferred currency, salespeople, and their assigned accounts categorized accordingly. When a salesperson associated with a certain market creates a proposal, they'll have access to all products and packages published on that market's Marketplace.
