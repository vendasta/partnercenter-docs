# Add accounts to a multi-location group

Adding accounts to a multi-location group will allow them to be viewed in [Multi-Location Business App](/business-app/multi-location-business-app).

1. [Go to **Partner Center**](https://partners.vendasta.com/dashboard?marketId=default)
2. [Go to **Accounts > Multi-location Groups**](https://partners.vendasta.com/brands/manage)
   ![Multi-location Groups menu](/img/business-app/multi-location/add-accounts/multi-location-groups.jpg)
3. Click on the group you'd like to add accounts to
4. Click **Add accounts**
   ![Add accounts button](/img/business-app/multi-location/add-accounts/add-accounts-button.png)
5. Click **+** next to each account you'd like to add
6. Click **Done**
   ![Done button](/img/business-app/multi-location/add-accounts/done-button.png)
