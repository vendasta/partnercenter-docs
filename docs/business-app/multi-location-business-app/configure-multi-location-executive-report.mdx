---
title: "How do I configure the Multi-location Executive report?"
sidebar_label: "Configure the Multi-location Executive Report"
---

The Multi-Location Executive Report compiles data from all individual locations and rolls them up into an easily digestible report. The report obeys the selections made within the **Partner Center > Accounts > Multi-Location Groups > *Group name* > Features** section.
