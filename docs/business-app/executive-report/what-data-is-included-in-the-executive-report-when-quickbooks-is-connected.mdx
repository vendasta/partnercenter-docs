---
title: What data is included in the Executive Report when QuickBooks is connected?
sidebar_label: QuickBooks Data in Executive Report
description: Information about the data included in the Executive Report when QuickBooks is connected
---

Once a user has connected their QuickBooks Online account in Business App, their Executive Report will include data on their income, expenses, and more.

![QuickBooks data in Executive Report](/img/business-app-executive-report/quickbooks-executive-report.jpg)

They will also receive in-platform and email notifications whenever one of their QuickBooks Online invoices has been paid in full.
