---
title: Reset passwords for Salespeople
description: Guide on how to reset passwords for Salespeople in the CRM
---

To reset a Salesperson's password:

1. Go to **Partner Center** > **CRM** > [**Salespeople**](https://partners.vendasta.com/st/manage-accounts).
2. Click on the name of the salesperson whose password needs to be reset.
3. Click **Send Reset Password Email.**
