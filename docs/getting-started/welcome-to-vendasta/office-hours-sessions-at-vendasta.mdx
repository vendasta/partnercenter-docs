We're excited to offer contracted partners the opportunity to connect directly with a Vendasta Customer Success Manager through our Office Hours.

This is your chance to get one-on-one support, answering your burning question or solving a pressing problem to keep you moving forward. Each session is private and dedicated just to you, so you can get the focused support you need.

**Weekly Schedule:**

- **Mondays:** 3pm - 4pm ET
- **Tuesdays:** 3pm - 5pm ET
- **Wednesdays:** 3pm - 5pm ET
- **Thursdays:** 3pm - 5pm ET
- **Fridays:** 3pm - 4pm ET

We look forward to helping you navigate your challenges and achieve your goals. Book your appointment today by [clicking here](https://meeting.calendarhero.com/meeting/new/618a8a7e94ee6b002094caa0/officehoursappointment) and let's get started!

_* Not available for partners on Free, Starter, or Startup subscriptions._
