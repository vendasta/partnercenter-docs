# The Vendasta Affiliate Program

Do you know of a marketing agency, A technology provider, a telecommunications company, or someone who is an expert in helping local businesses succeed? Diversify your revenue stream by earning up to 30% commission through our affiliate program.

### **How does the Affiliate Program work?**

#### **Sign up**

First, you'll need to sign up for the program. To do this: 

1. Go to [vendasta.com/affiliate](http://www.vendasta.com/affiliate)
2. Learn more about our program 
3. Click **Join Now**
4. Create account, then agree to the terms and conditions 

#### **Share**

Once you've signed up for the program, you'll be directed to your Affiliate Dashboard. Here, you'll find:

- Your custom referral link will direct your lead to our tracked landing page.
- Form to register direct leads.
- Stats - Clicks, referrals, customers, and earnings
- Resources
- Easy messaging 

### **How much can I earn?**

You will earn 20% on any paid subscription plan for your referral purchases, with the potential to move up to 30% commission by becoming a certified Vendasta affiliate. You can earn between $500 and $8,300 per referral over two years!

#### **Payment schedule**

**Monthly plans**

When a referred customer purchases a monthly pricing plan, you'll receive your commission on a month-to-month basis, paid out on the 13th of each month. 

For example, any commission or rewards that you earn in the month of August will be paid out on the 13th of September. 

**Annual plans**

When a referred customer purchases an annual pricing plan, you'll receive your lump-sum commission the following month on the 13th. 

For example, if a referred customer pays for a 1-year plan, you will receive a year 1 commission the following month on the 13th. Year 2 payment will be sent to you the month after their subscription renewal.
