### **Google Ads**

- Search Ads
- Display Ads
- YouTube Video Ads
- YouTube Search Ads
- Google Shopping Ads
- Gmail Ads

### **Facebook Ads**

- Facebook
- Instagram Ads
- Messenger Ads
- Audience Network Ads

In addition, there is a **Google Analytics** view that shows goal completions and top traffic sources.
