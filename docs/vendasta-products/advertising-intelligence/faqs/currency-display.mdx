---
title: "Which currency will be shown in the Advertising Intelligence dashboard?"
sidebar_label: "Currency Display"
description: "Information about how currency is displayed in the Advertising Intelligence dashboard"
---

The currency displayed in Advertising Intelligence is based on the currency that is set up on the connected Facebook or Google ad account. For example, if the Facebook ad account is set up in USD, then USD will be shown in Advertising Intelligence for Client CPC and Client Spend.
