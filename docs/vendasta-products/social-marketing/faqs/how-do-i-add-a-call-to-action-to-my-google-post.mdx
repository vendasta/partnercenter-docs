# How do I add a Call to Action to my Google post?

When composing a post in Social Marketing to a Google Business Profile account you will have the option to add a call to action. Expand the arrow next to Google Business Profile options, then add a call to action, select the CTA 'like Learn More', then input the Call-to-Action URL.
