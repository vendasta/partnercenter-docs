title: How do I find the bitrate of a video file?
sidebar_label: Find bitrate of a video file

---

Here is how to find the bitrate of a video file:

1. Right-click on the file.
2. Select **Properties**.
3. Then switch to the "Details" tab and check the "Video" section.
