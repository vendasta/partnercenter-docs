# What currencies can I accept with Vendasta Payments?

Vendasta Payments currently accepts payments in Canadian Dollars (CAD), US Dollars (USD), Australian Dollars (AUD), Great British Pounds (GBP), New Zealand Dollars (NZD), and Czech Koruna (CZK) currencies.
