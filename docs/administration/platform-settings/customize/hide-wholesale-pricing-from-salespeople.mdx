---
title: How do I hide wholesale pricing from my Salespeople?
sidebar_label: Hide wholesale pricing from Salespeople
description: Learn how to prevent your salespeople from seeing the wholesale pricing of marketplace products by changing settings in Partner Center.
---

You can prevent your salespeople from seeing the wholesale pricing of marketplace products by changing this setting in Partner Center.

To do so, go to Partner Center, then navigate to **Administration** > **Customize**.

Once in this area, you can expand the '**Sales**' section, scroll down, and toggle off **Show wholesale prices** to hide this pricing from your salespeople.
