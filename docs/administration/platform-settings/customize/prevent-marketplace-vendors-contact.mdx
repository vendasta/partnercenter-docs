# Is there a way to prevent Marketplace Vendors from directly contacting Partners?

Yes! You can opt out of any vendor communication by following these steps:

**Step 1:** From Partner Center, navigate to the **Administration** tab.

**Step 2:** Click on **Customize.**

**Step 3:** Under **Email Settings**, remove your email from 'Vendor Contact Email settings'.
