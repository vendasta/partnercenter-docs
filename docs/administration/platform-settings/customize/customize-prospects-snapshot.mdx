By default, your prospects can request access to Business App from the Snapshot Report. That way, they can explore the platform to discover all of its benefits first-hand. You have the flexibility to customize your prospects' experience from account creation to Business App access.

To customize your prospects' experience:

1. Go to **Partner Center > Administration > [Customize](https://partners.vendasta.com/customize-design?utm_campaign=partner-onboarding&utm_medium=referral&utm_source=resource-center&utm_content=customize-platform-inline-link)**.  
2. Click on the **Sales** section to expand the options.
3. Click **Edit Default Marketing and User Experience**.

![Customize menu showing the Sales section expanded with Edit Default Marketing and User Experience option](/img/customize-snapshot-experience/customize-menu.png)

4. Follow each of the steps in the wizard.
5. Click **Finish** (you may also preview first).
