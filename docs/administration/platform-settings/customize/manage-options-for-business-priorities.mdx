Partner Center Admins can add, edit, and delete options for main goals and training priorities. 

To manage options for main goals and training priorities:

1. Go to **Partner Center** > **Administration** > [**Customize**](https://partners.vendasta.com/customize-design).
2. Click on the **Sales** section to expand the options.
3. In the **Business Priorities** section, click **Edit Main Goals and Training Priorities**.

   ![Customize Sales Section](/img/business-priorities/customize-sales-section.jpg)

   ![Edit Main Goals](/img/business-priorities/edit-main-goals.jpg)

4. To create a Main Goal:
   1. Click **Add new**.
   2. Enter a title and description for the goal.
   3. Click **Create**.

5. To create a Training Priority:
   1. Click on the **Training Priorities** tab.

      ![Training Priorities Tab](/img/business-priorities/training-priorities-tab.jpg)

   2. Click **Add new**.
   3. Enter a title.
   4. Click **Create**.

6. To edit or delete an option, hover over the option, then click **Edit** or **Delete**.

   ![Edit or Delete Options](/img/business-priorities/edit-delete-options.jpg)
