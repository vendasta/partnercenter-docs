# Arrange your Store

Just like in physical stores, the presentation and display of products are crucial. It's important to highlight your best-selling and most significant packages as if they are MVPs. Therefore, you have the freedom to organize your store according to your preferences.

## How to edit the order of packages in your Store

To edit the order of packages in your Store:

1. Go to **Partner Center > Marketplace > Packages.**
2. Switch to the Grid layout.
3. Click **Edit Order.**
4. Select and drag the packages to reorder them as desired.
5. Click **Save Order.**

When customers and prospects open the business app store or store link, they will see the packages in the order that you arranged them.
