## How do I send SMS messages?

**Method 1:** Sending an SMS message to <u>one</u> new contact

This method will add a new contact to your Customers list.

1. On the overview tab of **Customer Voice,** click **Send request**.
2. Fill in the contact form. *Note:* You must include a phone number to select an SMS-method template to send.
3. Select an SMS template from the dropdown.
4. Click **Send**.

[Watch the video here.](#video-tutorial)

**Method 2:** Sending an SMS message to <u>one or more</u> existing contacts

1. **Overview page** > **Customers**
2. Select one or more customers > **Send request.** *Note:* All the selected customers must have a phone number.
3. Select an SMS template from the list to preview > click **Send to [#] Customers.**

**\*Only available with SMS add-ons.**
