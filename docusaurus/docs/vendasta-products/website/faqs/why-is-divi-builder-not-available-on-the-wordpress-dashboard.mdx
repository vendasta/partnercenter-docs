---
title: Why is Divi builder not available on the WordPress Dashboard?
sidebar_label: Divi builder not available
description: Learn why the Divi builder might not be available on your WordPress Dashboard
---

Divi builder comes by default **only** for select themes in Website Standard/Pro, therefore when selecting a theme, make sure to choose a theme that supports the DIVI builder. 

Themes that support DIVI builder will have a yellow star displayed in the top right corner.

![Themes with Divi builder support have a yellow star in the top right corner](./img/vendasta-products-website-faqs/divi-builder-theme-yellow-star.png)
