In addition to publishing to Instagram, you can also see comments on posts and respond to them directly within Social Marketing.

Engaging with customers is one of the key factors of a successful social media strategy. Now, your clients can interact with their audience on one of the largest social networks: Instagram!

Any comments made on your clients' Instagram posts will be pulled into the **Comments** section under **Tools.**

To respond to a specific customer, use the **Comment** button. Their Instagram handle will automatically be populated, just type your response and click **Post**.
