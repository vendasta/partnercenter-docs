---
title: How to Disable Automatic Monthly Reports in Task Manager
sidebar_position: 1
---

If you'd prefer not to send automatic monthly reports in Task Manager, follow these steps:

1. Log in to the **Partner Center**.
2. Navigate to **Fulfillment** and select **Open Task Manager**.
3. In Task Manager, go to the **Accounts** tab and choose the account you wish to update.
4. Once in the account, click on the **Stats & Reports** tab.
5. Locate the toggle labeled **Automatically Send Report Monthly** and switch it off.

After completing these steps, automatic monthly reports for the selected account will no longer be sent.
