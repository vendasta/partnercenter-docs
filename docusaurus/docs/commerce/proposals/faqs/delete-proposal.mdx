---
title: How do I delete a proposal?
description: Learn how to archive proposals in Vendasta's Commerce module when they are no longer needed.
sidebar_label: Deleting Proposals
---

Once a Proposal has been created, it cannot be deleted.

You can, however, archive it by clicking the **Archive** button on the right side of the Manage Proposals page.

![Archive button on the Manage Proposals page](./img/commerce/proposals/archive-proposal.jpg)
