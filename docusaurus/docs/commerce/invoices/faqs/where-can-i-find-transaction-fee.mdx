---
title: Where can I find the transaction fee charged on customer payment?
sidebar_position: 14
description: Learn how to find transaction fees for customer payments in the Vendasta Platform
---

Partners can go into each payment individually in **Partner Center** > **Commerce** > **Payments** > Click on a Payment to view the associated transaction fees.
