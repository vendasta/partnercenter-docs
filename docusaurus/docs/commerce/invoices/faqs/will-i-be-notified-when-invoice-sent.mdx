---
title: Will I be notified when an invoice is sent to my customer?
description: Information about invoice notification emails for both merchants and their customers.
sidebar_label: Invoice Notifications
---

**Yes!** You will receive an email every time an invoice is generated and sent to your customer.

![Invoice notification email sent to merchant](./img/commerce/invoices/invoice-notification-email-1.jpg)

The customer will also receive an email invoice (if you have chosen the payment option to **Email Invoice**). Otherwise, the customer will receive an email receipt for the charge to their Credit Card.

![Invoice email sent to customer](./img/commerce/invoices/invoice-notification-email-2.jpg)

![Invoice receipt email sent to customer](./img/commerce/invoices/invoice-notification-email-3.jpg)
