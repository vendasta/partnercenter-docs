# Form Builder Overview

Learn about Marketing Forms to create lead capture forms for your website.

## Overview

Marketing Forms is a tool that allows you to create customizable lead capture forms to embed on your website. Forms can be used to collect prospect information, generate leads, and engage with your audience.

## Key Features

### Drag-and-Drop Form Builder

The intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to create professional forms without any coding knowledge. Simply add fields, customize their properties, and arrange them in your preferred order.

### Multiple Field Types

Choose from a variety of field types to collect the information you need:

- Text fields (short and long)
- Email address
- Phone number
- Name fields
- Dropdown menus
- Checkboxes
- Radio buttons
- File uploads
- Date and time pickers
- Hidden fields

### Form Templates

Start with pre-designed templates for common use cases:

- Contact forms
- Lead generation forms
- Newsletter signup
- Event registration
- Job applications
- Surveys and feedback

### Customizable Styling

Match your brand's look and feel by customizing:

- Colors and fonts
- Button styles
- Field layouts
- Background images
- Custom CSS (for advanced users)

### Mobile Responsiveness

All forms automatically adjust to display correctly on any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.

### Form Logic

Create dynamic forms with conditional logic:

- Show or hide fields based on previous answers
- Skip to specific sections based on user input
- Validate fields with custom rules

### Spam Protection

Keep your forms secure with built-in spam protection:

- reCAPTCHA integration
- Honeypot fields
- Rate limiting

### Submission Handling

Choose what happens after a form is submitted:

- Custom thank you messages
- Redirect to specific pages
- Download files or resources
- Show confirmation details

### Integrations

Connect your forms with other tools and platforms:

- CRM systems
- Email marketing platforms
- Google Analytics
- Zapier for hundreds of other integrations

### Analytics and Reporting

Track form performance with detailed analytics:

- Submission rates
- Conversion tracking
- Field completion statistics
- Abandonment data

## Getting Started

To begin creating forms:

1. Navigate to **Marketing** > **Forms** in your dashboard
2. Click **Create Form** to start building a new form
3. Select a template or start from scratch
4. Add and customize your form fields
5. Configure form settings and submission handling
6. Publish and embed your form on your website
