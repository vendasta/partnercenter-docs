---
title: Where can I change my Campaign outgoing Email and Name Settings?
description: You can change the 'sender' email address and name in Partner Center > Marketing > Email Settings > Sender and Reply Settings.
sidebar_label: Campaign Email and Name Settings
---

You can change the 'sender' email address and name in **Partner Center** > **Marketing** > **Email Settings > Sender and Reply Settings.**
