# My Account

In this section, you'll find all the information you need to manage your Vendasta account settings. Navigate through the following subsections to find specific guidance:

## Available Sections

- [My Plan](/administration/my-account/my-plan) - View and manage your subscription plan and available features
- [My Team](/administration/my-account/my-team) - Manage your team members, roles, and permissions
- [My Billing](/administration/my-account/my-billing) - Review billing information, invoices, and payment methods
- [Affiliate Program](/administration/my-account/affiliate-program) - Learn about Vendasta's affiliate program and how to participate
