---
title: How do I change the email address I want my invoices sent to?
description: Learn how to update the email address where your invoices are sent in the Partner Center.
sidebar_position: 2
---

Updating the email address that we send invoices to is as easy as updating the Billing Contact information under **Partner Center > Administration > My Billing > Edit Billing contact**.

![Edit Billing Contact screen](./img/administration/my-account/my-billing/edit-billing-contact.jpg)
