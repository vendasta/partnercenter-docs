---
title: Can multiple credit cards be added to Partner Center?
slug: /administration/my-account/my-billing/can-multiple-credit-cards-be-added-to-partner-center
---

No, there can only be **one credit card** added to Partner Center. You can add or edit your payment method by going to **Administration > My Billing > Add Payment Method.**

![Screenshot showing Administration > My Billing > Add Payment Method](/img/administration/my-account/my-billing/add-payment-method.png)

Any attempt to add a second card will overwrite the previous one.
