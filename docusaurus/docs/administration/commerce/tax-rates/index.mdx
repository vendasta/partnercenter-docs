# Tax Management

Tax management enables you to configure tax rates, apply exemptions, and ensure accurate tax calculations on invoices, sales orders, and subscription billing. The system handles complex tax rules and automatically applies them to transactions.

## Available Articles

- [Tax Rates and Exemption Management](./tax-configuration-guide.md) - Complete guide to configuring tax rates, managing exemptions, and handling tax calculations

This comprehensive guide covers all tax management functionality, including rate configuration, item and account exemptions, and address-based tax logic with integrated frequently asked questions.

If you need additional assistance with tax configuration, please contact support through your account dashboard.
