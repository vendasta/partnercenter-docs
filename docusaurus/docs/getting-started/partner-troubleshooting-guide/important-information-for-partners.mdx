# The Vendasta Affiliate Program

Do you know of a marketing agency, a technology provider, a telecommunications company, or someone who is an expert in helping local businesses succeed? Diversify your revenue stream by earning up to 30% commission through our affiliate program.

### How does the Affiliate Program work?

#### Sign up

1. Go to `http://www.vendasta.com/affiliate`
2. Learn more about our program
3. Click Join Now
4. Create an account, then agree to the terms and conditions

#### Share

Once you've signed up for the program, you'll be directed to your Affiliate Dashboard. Here, you'll find:

- Your custom referral link will direct your lead to our tracked landing page.
- Form to register direct leads.
- Stats — Clicks, referrals, customers, and earnings
- Resources
- Easy messaging

### How much can I earn?

You will earn 20% on any paid subscription plan your referral purchases, with the potential to move up to 30% commission by becoming a certified Vendasta affiliate. You can earn between $500 and $8,300 per referral over two years!

#### Payment schedule

Monthly plans: When a referred customer purchases a monthly pricing plan, you'll receive your commission on a month-to-month basis, paid out on the 13th of each month.

Annual plans: When a referred customer purchases an annual pricing plan, you'll receive your lump-sum commission the following month on the 13th.

## FAQs

<details>
<summary>Who is the ideal partner for Vendasta?</summary>

Vendasta is looking for marketers eager to optimize their tech stack and streamline operations for maximum efficiency.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How will I know if my application to become a referral partner has been accepted?</summary>

We will be in contact with you within 3 working days to discuss whether your application has been approved or rejected.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I refer a prospective customer?</summary>

Log into PartnerStack and share your custom link or complete the Referral form.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What sales enablement tools are available?</summary>

We provide resources and assets including onboarding decks, videos, banner ads, platform features, photos, email templates, best practices, and more.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long do commissions last for?</summary>

Commissions last for up to 2 years.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I get paid?</summary>

At the end of each month, rewards are calculated and available for cash‑out via PayPal or Stripe (alternative methods may be available for non‑PayPal regions).

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long do cookies last for?</summary>

We use cookies to track referrals. Cookies last for 90 days after someone clicks on your link. If they click again, the 90 days restarts.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is PartnerStack?</summary>

PartnerStack provides the tools and resources needed to promote our product and rewards you for it.

</details>
