# Why can't I access my premium report?

**Q: After being given access to view a specific report, I am getting a 401 No Access error message. Why is this?**

A: This is like an issue to do with cookies. Your browser may be automatically blocking certain third-party cookies that are needed to access the report. To fix this, please enable third-party cookie access in your browser settings.
