---
title: Where can Salespeople see their upcoming meetings?
description: Find out where to view scheduled meetings in Partner Center
sidebar_position: 6
---

Upcoming meetings are visible in Partner Center via **CRM > My Meetings > Your Scheduled Events.**

- Toggle between tabs to see upcoming or past meetings.
- Add a filter to view meetings by time length.
